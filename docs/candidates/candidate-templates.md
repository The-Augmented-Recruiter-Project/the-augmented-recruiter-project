# Candidate templates and request examples

These templates are designed to be:
- legally grounded
- neutral in tone
- proportionate
- easy to adapt

They are not legal threats.

---

## Template: Asking whether AI or automation was used

Subject: Clarification regarding application processing

Hello,

I am writing to ask for clarification regarding the processing of my recent application.

Could you please confirm whether automated processing or profiling was used as part of the screening or decision-making process, and whether the decision involved human review?

Thank you for your time.

Kind regards,  
[Name]

---

## Template: Requesting information about personal data

Subject: Request for information about personal data processing

Hello,

I would like to request information about the personal data held about me in relation to my application, including:
- categories of data processed
- whether automated processing was involved
- retention period for this data

Thank you for your assistance.

Best regards,  
[Name]

---

## Template: Follow-up on vague responses

Hello,

Thank you for your response.

To clarify, could you please confirm whether the decision was made solely through automated processing, or whether a human reviewer was involved at any stage?

I appreciate your clarification.

Kind regards,  
[Name]

---

## How to use these templates

- Adjust tone to match the organisation
- Keep requests specific
- Avoid accusations
- Do not escalate prematurely

These templates aim to maximise clarity, not confrontation.
