# Regulatory Scope Reference (EU AI Act)

This page provides a **non-exhaustive, non-interpretive reference** to where the EU AI Act discusses the use of AI systems in employment and recruitment contexts.

It exists to help readers locate relevant sections of the law.

This project does **not** provide legal advice or compliance guidance.

---

## How to read this page

- This page maps **common hiring activities** to **where they are referenced in the EU AI Act**
- Descriptions are intentionally neutral and minimal
- Readers should consult the original legal text for authoritative meaning

---

## Hiring stages and regulatory references

| Hiring stage | Why referenced in the AI Act | Relevant section |
|-------------|------------------------------|------------------|
| Role definition & job design | Systems used to define or influence employment criteria may affect access to employment | Annex III — High-risk AI systems (Employment) |
| Salary benchmarking & compensation | Systems influencing pay and employment conditions are linked to employment decision-making | Annex III — High-risk AI systems (Employment) |
| Outbound sourcing | Systems used to identify or target candidates may affect access to employment | Annex III — High-risk AI systems (Employment) |
| Inbound sourcing & application filtering | Systems used to screen or filter applications directly affect access to employment | Annex III — High-risk AI systems (Employment) |
| Screening & pre-selection | Explicitly referenced as high-risk when used for employment-related decisions | Annex III — High-risk AI systems (Employment) |
| Interview support | Systems influencing assessment or comparison of candidates relate to employment evaluation | Annex III — High-risk AI systems (Employment) |
| References & background checks | Systems used to evaluate candidate suitability relate to employment decisions | Annex III — High-risk AI systems (Employment) |
| Referrals & internal mobility | Systems influencing promotion or reassignment are included in employment contexts | Annex III — High-risk AI systems (Employment) |
